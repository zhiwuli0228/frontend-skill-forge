## Key Findings

1. The self-evolution loop works end-to-end — Evidence → Archive completed in one V7 iteration.
2. AntD Button.Group → Space.Compact is a safe, low-risk migration that makes a good controlled drift source.
3. Testid renames during library migration are the most common silent failure — RE-006 addresses this.
4. Knowledge maps and evolution queues provide a durable mechanism for tracking drift → candidate → patch.
5. Minimal patches (2 lines) were sufficient to fix the failure — over-patching is a risk in evolution cycles.
